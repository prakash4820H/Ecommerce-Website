About THIS™

Welcome to THIS™, where we are redefining the plant-based revolution with innovative, sustainable, and mouthwatering meat alternatives. We’re not just a food brand—we’re a movement committed to changing the way the world thinks about food, health, and the environment.

Our Story

It all began with a bold idea: to create a product that challenges the norms of traditional meat while delivering an authentic taste and texture that meat-lovers crave. Our flagship product, “THIS™ Isn't Chicken Thighs,” was born out of countless hours of research, testing, and passion for sustainable food innovation.

"We're here to prove that sustainable, plant-based alternatives can be just as delicious, indulgent, and satisfying as the real thing."

Our Mission

At THIS™, our mission is to empower you to enjoy food without compromise. By combining cutting-edge culinary science with natural, high-quality ingredients, we strive to provide products that are not only healthier for you but also kinder to our planet. We believe that every bite can make a difference.

Our Process

Our production process is rooted in innovation and sustainability. We source the finest natural ingredients, using state-of-the-art technology to create textures and flavors that mimic traditional meat. Our approach minimizes waste and reduces environmental impact, ensuring that every product is a step towards a greener future.

Innovation & Quality

Our relentless pursuit of excellence drives us to continuously innovate. From perfecting our flavor profiles to enhancing the nutritional value of our products, we push the boundaries of what plant-based foods can achieve. Quality is our hallmark, and we are dedicated to providing you with products that exceed your expectations.

Our Community

At the heart of THIS™ is a vibrant community of passionate food enthusiasts, environmental advocates, and innovators. We’re proud to be part of a growing movement that is reshaping the culinary landscape and paving the way for a healthier, more sustainable future. Join us, and be part of a revolution that matters.

Looking Ahead

The journey has just begun. As we continue to expand our product range and explore new culinary frontiers, our commitment to quality, sustainability, and innovation remains unwavering. We invite you to explore our products, share your experiences, and join us in creating a better future—one revolutionary bite at a time.
